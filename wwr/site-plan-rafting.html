Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show people where the rafting business is located and also on what rivers they operate. It's purpose is also to show at what times of the year the rafting business is operating. It allows the user ease of access to needed info and should give the user a way to contact the business.

Audience

The target audience for a white water rafting website is people who enjoy outdoor activies. This can include very active adventurers and also beginners who are just barely stepping out into the great outdoors. They can be young or old.
